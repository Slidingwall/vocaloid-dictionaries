See also:[Symbol Charts](/vocaloid-dictionaries/symbol-charts)

> `ENG` = English X-SAMPA  
> `KOR` = Korean X-SAMPA  
> `ESP` = Spanish X-SAMPA  
> `MAI` = Extended Spanish X-SAMPA for Maika
