# Chinese 中文

{: .new-title }
> List of Abbreviations / 简称列表
>
> `CHN` = Chinese X-SAMPA  
> `IPA` = International Phonetic Alphabet  
> `ENG` = English X-SAMPA  
> `JPN` = Japanese X-SAMPA
> `ESP` = Spanish X-SAMPA  
> `MAI` = Extended Spanish X-SAMPA for Maika
