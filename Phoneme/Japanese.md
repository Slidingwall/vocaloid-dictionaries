# Japanese 日语

{: .new-title }
> List of Abbreviations / 简称列表
>
> &#96;JPN&#96; = Japanese X-SAMPA
> &#96;CHN&#96; = Chinese X-SAMPA  
> &#96;KOR&#96; = Korean X-SAMPA  
> &#96;ESP&#96; = Spanish X-SAMPA  
> &#96;MAI&#96; = Extended Spanish X-SAMPA for Maika

| **N  N\\** | [^1] | Np | n | N |
