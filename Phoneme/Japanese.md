# Japanese 日语

{: .new-title }
> List of Abbreviations / 简称列表
>
> `JPN` = Japanese X-SAMPA  
> `IPA` = International Phonetic Alphabet  
> `CHN` = Chinese X-SAMPA  
> `KOR` = Korean X-SAMPA  
> `ESP` = Spanish X-SAMPA  
> `MAI` = Extended Spanish X-SAMPA for Maika

| **e** |e̞| ei | e | e | e |
| **o** |o̞| @U | o | o | o |
| **N  N\\** |ŋ ɴ| [^1] | Np | n | N |
| **N'** |ŋʲ| [^1] | Np | J | J |
